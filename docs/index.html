Privacy Policy for Money Tracker

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Money Tracker ("the app") is a personal finance tracking app built by Subhradeep Kundu. This policy explains what happens to your data when you use the app.

Data collection

Money Tracker does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. The app has no backend, no user accounts, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Where your data lives

All information you enter — accounts, categories, transactions, and budgets — is stored only on your device, using local on-device storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself.

Backup and export

The app includes an optional JSON backup/export feature. When you use it, a file containing your data is created and shared through your device's native share sheet (for example, to save it to a file, cloud drive, or messaging app of your choice). This action is entirely under your control — the app itself does not upload or transmit this file anywhere.

Third parties

Money Tracker does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties because no data ever leaves your device through the app itself.

Data deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data/storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
